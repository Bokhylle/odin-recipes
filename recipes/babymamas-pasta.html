Baby-Mama's Favourite Pasta

Sure fire hit with any baby mama

Make sure to make enough as any expecting mother will crave for more and you better not run out!


I came up with this recipe while cooking a Slow Curry for baby mama

Ingredients:

  • Ghee
  • Curry powder (I used standard hot curry powder from the super market
  • Other powdered spices to your preference
  • Good quality tomato paste (I used Mutti)
  • Optional:A simple protein to your preference

Yes that is actually all you'll need!!

Steps:

  1. gently cook powdered spices in a pre-heated pan on low heat for 30s until fragrant
  2. Add your ghee and stirr to mix, increase heat to medium
  3. Add your tomato paste and stir in untill well mixed and cook until the paste looks pitted
  4. Spread over rice and enjoy
