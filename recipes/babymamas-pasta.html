Baby-Mama's Favourite Pasta

Sure fire hit with any baby mama

Make sure to make enough as any expecting mother will crave for more and you better not run out!


This is my simple take on a classic italian tomato sauce

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Pecorino Romano, or any hard parmesan-like cheese
  • Good rough surfaced pasta
  • Powdered chicken stock
  • Herbs of choice
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional:A simple protein to your preference

Yes that is actually all you'll need!!

Steps:

  1. Start heating your salted pasta water and add pasta to pot when it start to boil
  2. Heat oil in a non stick pan on medium heat and add onion. It should start sizzling as it hits the pan. Stir to coat
  3. Cook for 3-5 min until slightly translucent and add garlic. Stir and cook for about 30s
  4. Spread onion and garlic to the edges and place tomato paste in middle of pan and stir gently until cooked without mixing
  5. Meanwhile, strain your pasta and save a cup of the starchy pasta water
  6. Stir onions garlic and tomato paste together and shake some chicken stock powder over the top
  7. Pour in the pasta water and stir until it thickens slighly. Leave to simmer for a few minutes until it reaches your preferred consitency
  8. Remove from heat and add pasta to pan. Stir to coat and grate the Pecorino over top. Serve immediately
