Simple Pizza Sauce

Want pizza? This sauce will do the trick.

Just bring your own base and you are set to go!


Also doubles as a delicious dipping sauce..

Ingredients:

  • Passata
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt and herbs to taste

Yes that is actually all you'll need!!

Steps:

  1. Chop Garlic with some salt and place in small bowl and add passata
  2. Add oil and lemon juice
  3. Stir until oil is incorporated in sauce
  4. Salt and herbs to taste
  5. Place in fridge for 30min+ to let flavours develop
  6. Your sauce is ready to use. Spread over a base and cook on a preheated pizza stone or enjoy as a dip to your favourite fried goods!
